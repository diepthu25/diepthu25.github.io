Cocktails

Vietnamese cocktails, blending local fruits, herbs, and spices with traditional spirits, create a refreshing fusion of tropical and exotic flavors, perfect for any occasion.

Vietnamese coffee is known for its bold, robust flavor, often brewed using a traditional phin filter that allows the coffee to drip slowly, creating a rich and intense taste. This unique brewing process, combined with sweet condensed milk, offers a perfect harmony of bitterness and sweetness that has become iconic. Whether enjoyed in a bustling street café or at home, every sip of Vietnamese coffee is a journey through the country’s vibrant culture and coffee heritage

Frequently Asked Questions !

Do you offer any discounts or promotions?

We regularly offer discounts and promotions. Check our website or subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on the latest deals and offers.

Can I place an order for pickup?

Yes, you can place an order for pickup. During checkout, select the pickup option and choose the time that is most convenient for you. You will receive a notification when your order is ready for pickup.

How do I book a table?

To book a table, navigate to the 'Table' section on our website. Select your preferred date and time, provide the necessary details, and confirm your booking. You will receive a confirmation email with your booking details.
